E-commerce Store

2023 website Dashboard, User Experience Design

This eCommerce Management platform is designed to streamline the operations of online stores and provide a comprehensive solution for managing the entire sales process.

Product Management:

Enables store owners to easily add, edit, and remove products, manage stock levels, set pricing, and apply discounts or promotions. Product categories and attributes are customizable for better organization.

Dashboard:

Offers an overview of key business metrics, including total sales, active orders, revenue growth, customer insights, and inventory levels. Administrators can monitor real-time data to make informed business decisions.

Analytics and Reports:

Offers detailed sales reports, inventory insights, and customer behavior analysis. These reports help businesses optimize performance, forecast demand, and make data-driven decisions.
